Sinead O'Connor Has Sung &quot;Nothing Compares 2 U&quot; for the Last Time

January 19, 2024
blog

Say it isn't so! Sinead O'Connor announced via Facebook this week that she plans to stop performing her most famous and beloved song, the '90s classic "Nothing Compares 2 U," in any and all future concerts.

"OK, the time has come for me to cease singing Nothing Compares 2 U," the Irish-born chanteuse wrote on Monday, March 16. "The first principle of the manner in which I'm trained as a singer (Bel Canto) is we never sing a song we don't emotionally identify with. After twenty-five years of singing it, nine months or so ago I finally ran out of anything I could use in order to bring some emotion to it." 

Because of that, she feels she'd be doing a disservice by continuing to perform the tune. She understands, though, that it's a very big part of her history — and of her fans' history with her music. 

"I don't want audiences to be disappointed coming along to a show and then not hearing it, so am letting you know here that you won't," she wrote. "If I were to sing it just to please people, I wouldn't be doing my job right, because my job is to be emotionally available. I'd be lying. You'd be getting a lie."

She continued: "My job is to give you honesty. I'm trained in honesty. I can't act. It just isn't in my training. I have ceased singing other songs over the years for the same reason."

O'Connor released "Nothing Compares 2 U" in 1990, as a single from her second studio album, I Do Not Want What I Haven't Got. The music video for the song — which memorably featured close-ups of O'Connor's face — won three MTV Video Music Awards for Best Female Video, Best Post-Modern Video, and Video of the Year.
