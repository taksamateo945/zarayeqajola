Kylie Jenner Shows Off $2.7 Million House on App, Has Tyga's Mugshot

July 15, 2024

Kylie Jenner treated fans to a tour of her newly renovated $2.7 million Calabasas, Calif., mansion — which features a framed mugshot of boyfriend Tyga in pride of place!

Jenner, 18, bought her house eight months ago and, through her website and app Thekyliejenner.com, showed some very personal items on Friday, Oct. 9.

Prominently displayed on a shelf is a framed mugshot photo of Tyga, her boyfriend, from his arrest three years ago. The photo of the rapper is placed on the shelf directly below a framed photo of her grandmother Esther. Next to the photo of Tyga is a statue of her dog Norman.

The 18-year-old, who was sporting Adidas trainers and a casual but cute blue T-shirt as she gave the tour, also hung artwork from her Kylie Lip Kit packaging in her living room.
