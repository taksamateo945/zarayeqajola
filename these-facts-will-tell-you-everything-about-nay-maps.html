Nay Maps Is A Multi-Talented Celebrity

Nay Maps is one South African entertainer with many exceptional talents, all of which have helped him maximize his potentials and make a great career in the entertainment industry.

Though professionally known as Nay Maps, this hardworking young man was named Nkosinathi Maphalala by his parents after his birth. He is easily recognized as an actor, but apart from his acting talents, he makes extra cash from his music career, business venture, TV presentation job, and entrepreneur.

Nay has had an amazing mixed career over the years. He has worked with notable people both within and outside the South African entertainment world. His talents have also taken him to places across the country. In fact, it’s been an awesome journey so far for the young man.

He Hails From Gauteng Province

Nay Maps calls Gauteng province home. He was born in the region, precisely in Pretoria, on the 30th of March, 1989. The actor was raised in his hometown, after which he left the area for greener pastures in the city.

Maps had his primary and secondary school education at Sunnyside Primary School and Lyttelton Manor High School, respectively, in Pretoria. He graduated from the University of Pretoria, where he studied Information technology, programming, and software.

Not much is known about his life before he became famous. However, it is believed that it was his love for arts that motivated him to make a career switch in his early days.

Nay Maps Has A Great Relationship With His Family

The actor is blessed with an amazing support system and lovely people in his life. His family has been supportive right from the beginning of his career, and they have never relented in proving their love for him.

A Look At The Actor’s Biggest Project And Journey To Greatness

Though Nay studied Information technology, programming, and software, he has always wanted to build a career in front of the camera. Obviously, he and his siblings inherited their music gene from their parents – Bishop Simeon’s wife has motivated the actor towards developing an undying love for music.

The particular age Maps launched his career is unknown. However, records show that he earned his breakthrough on television while presenting on the ANN7 channel lifestyle show, Hashtag.

As an actor, the Pretoria native has appeared in several productions, including Uzalo Season 1 – 4 (as Mxolisi Xulu), Impilo: The Scam – Season 1 (as Pacino), Isibaya – Season 2 Tshepo (as Nay Maphalala), Skeem Saam – Season 8 as Mxolisi, as well as The Queen – Season 4 (as Dingane Khoza). His biggest project remains the South African TV series, Uzalo. The series brought so much fame and fortune on his way, and all the credit goes to his outstanding performance therein. Indeed, Mr. Maphalala put in his best in the project, and it panned out really nice.

His character (Xulu) is an unwilling young man who has to go on a journey of discovery from being a good boy to being the leader of his father’s shady business. Interestingly, Nay Maps has always done some modeling jobs and also made appearances in some commercials.
