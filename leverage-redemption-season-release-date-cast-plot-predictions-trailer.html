Leverage: Redemption Season 2 Storyline: What is it about?

Leverage: Redemption is back with a different storyline than the Leverage series aired in 2008. It consists of four masterminds: A hitter, A con artist, A cat burglar, and a hacker who join forces to make the rich who have always gotten away with their crimes pay for their misdeeds. Since their last job as society’s watchdog eight years ago, the rich have taken advantage of abusing the system to get away with their crimes while accumulating wealth. The team partners with Harry Wilson), a Lawyer who seeks redemption after realizing he has not been playing fair to uphold justice. Breanna Cassey, Harry Wilson’s sister, also a computer genius, gets involved in the mix.
