Alesso, Bazzi, Illenium and Rae Sremmurd to Headline X Games Aspen 2020 Celeb Secrets

February 22, 2024
blog

The music lineup for X Games Aspen 2020 has been revealed!

As if we’re not excited already for one of the biggest winter action sports events to hit Buttermilk Mountain, ESPN and X Games revealed the music lineup for this January’s festivities and let’s just say one thing… it’s epic. 

This year, the lineup is lead by Grammy-nominated producer and electronic music titan Alesso, who will be taking the stage on Saturday (January 25). Larger than life hop-hop duo Rae Sremmurd will make their X Games Aspen debut on Friday (January 24), ILLENIUM will deliver aural arrangements that vary in style, complexity and emotional depth on Saturday (January 25) and R&B/Pop superstar Bazzi will round out the lineup with his captivating lyricism and infectious melodies.

Tickets for X Games Aspen 2020 offer fans a unique experiential fusion of globally leading action sports competition and world-class musical offerings at the GEICO Music Stage. While admission to the action sports competitions and festival village are free to the public, tickets to X Games musical performances start at just $25. Fans wanting to experience all four days of action sports and music, an all-music GA ticket is available for $120. Fans purchasing before Dec. 5 will have the opportunity to access the best ticket-price options at xgames.com.

Belly Up Aspen, in partnership with X Games, will also host ILLENIUM on Wednesday 1/22, The Chainsmokers on Thursday 1/23, Alesso on Friday 1/24, Rae Sremmurd on Saturday 1/25 and Bazzi on Sunday 1/26.  X Games weekend marks 15 years of live music at the iconic venue and tickets will be available beginning tomorrow at bellyupaspen.com.

X Games Aspen takes place at the base of Buttermilk Mountain from Thursday, January 23 to Sunday, January 26.
