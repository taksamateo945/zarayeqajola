April 14, 2024

To celebrate the release of Little Mix’s Platinum edition of Glory Days on Friday (24th), we’ve created two quizzes based on the album. The first of which will be published today and the second on Wednesday. Today’s quiz will be testing your lyric knowledge of the platinum edition tracks!

Glory Days: The Platinum edition will feature all the tracks on the number one album alongside new tracks ‘If I Get My Way’, ‘Is My Love Enough?’ and ‘Dear Lover’. It will also include their collaborations with Kid Ink on ‘Touch, CNCO on ‘Reggaetón Lento (Remix)’, Machine Gun Kelly on ‘No More Sad Songs’, Stormzy on ‘Power’ and Charlie Puth on ‘Oops’. Find out more about the tracklisting here.

If that wasn’t enough there will also be a DVD released with the CD featuring the music videos for their recent singles and more excitingly the Glory Days: The Documentary. Having seen teaser clips of the documentary on Little Mix Twitters/Instagrams we are SO ready for the full thing!
