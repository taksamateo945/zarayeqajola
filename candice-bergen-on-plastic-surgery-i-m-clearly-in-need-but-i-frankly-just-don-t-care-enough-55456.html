Rene Zellwegers Appearance Looks Nearly Back to Normal at Charity Event

March 16, 2024
blog

Renée Zellweger looks like her old self again!

After receiving criticism for her drastically altered appearance at the ELLE Women in Hollywood Awards last fall, the 46-year-old actress looked happy and healthy at last night’s SeriousFun Children’s Network charity event in LA.

Renée on May 14, 2015.

Months ago, many speculated Renée had undergone extensive plastic surgery when she stepped out looking unrecognizable due to her noticeably taut and wrinkle-free face. Soon after, the actress shot back at her critics and claimed her new look wasn’t caused by cosmetic procedures.

“I’m glad folks think I look different! I’m living a different, happy, more fulfilling life, and I’m thrilled that perhaps it shows,” she previously told People in response to rumors that she had gone under the knife.

Renée in the early 1990s (left) and in October 2014 (right). (Photo Credits: Getty Images)

Last month, the Bridget Jones’s Diary star — whose May 2005 marriage to singer Kenny Chesney was annulled that following September — credited much of her newfound happiness to her boyfriend, musician Doyle Bramhall, 46. “He’s a very sweet man,” she gushed to People during an interview on Monday, April 20. “I’m very, very happy right now.”
