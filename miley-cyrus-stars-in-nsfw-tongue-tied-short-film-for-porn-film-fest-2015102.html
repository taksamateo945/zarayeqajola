Miley Cyrus' NSFW Short Film Won't Be Featured in Porn Film Fest

June 27, 2024
blog

Hannah who? Miley Cyrus expanded her range in a predictably scandalous yet artistic way this week. 

The “Adore You” singer, 22, was linked to the skin flick industry when a short film she made previously — Tongue Tied — was revealed to be included in the upcoming New York City Porn Festival. An earlier version of this story suggested she had entered it in the festival, but her rep tells Us that’s not the case. In fact, the film won’t be shown at all at the festival. “Miley was proud to make a film with acclaimed filmmaker Quentin Jones,” her rep says. “Neither she nor Quentin are participating in this ‘festival’ in any way.”

The former Hannah Montana star leads the black-and-white clip, which comes in at just under three minutes. Originally released in May 2014, Tongue Tied shows Cyrus in various stages of bondage and undress, with pasties prominently featured, as alt-J‘s “Fitzpleasure” plays.

Held in Brooklyn from Feb. 27 to March 1, the NYC Porn Film Festival was going to feature Cyrus’ video alongside other submissions. According to the schedule, which also includes a video from reality star Tila Tequila, an extended four-minute version of Cyrus’ project was scheduled to air.
