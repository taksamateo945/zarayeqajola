Lisa D'Amato, America's Next Top Model Winner, Bares Baby Bump

March 29, 2024
blog

If you've got it, flaunt it! America's Next Top Model All-Stars winner Lisa D'Amato bared her big and beautiful baby bump at a party celebrating the franchise's 20th cycle on Aug. 7 at the Supperclub in Hollywood.

The mom-to-be, 31, strutted her stuff in a super-cropped long-sleeved top with cutouts at the shoulders and a low-slung pleated maxi skirt. Statement earrings and an oversized hair bow completed her black-carpet look.

The model, who's married to entrepreneur Adam Friedman, spoke to Us Weekly about her rough pregnancy thus far. "Pregnancy doesn't go very well with me," she said.  "I'm nauseous every single day, and you know I'm just tired all the time."

Thankfully, there are plenty of good moments as well: "The coolest part of pregnancy is when you feel him moving and kicking," she told Us. "So once that started happening I was like, well at least there's something fun about this!"

Back in May, D'Amato had expressed to E! News her plans to stay fit during her pregnancy ("so I don't turn into a Cinnabon and can hopefully be a MILF in a month's time," she explained then). And it sounds like she's still on track to fulfilling her dream of being a hot mom. "I'm still in the gym for two and a half hours, four days a week — low impact of course," she said at Wednesday's event. "I think that it's very important, especially for someone with my genes, I mean the women, they're just done when they have kids, and I'm not going to let that happen!"

D'Amato is expecting to deliver her baby boy in "end of September, beginning of October," she shared.
