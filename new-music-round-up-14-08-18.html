New Music Round Up: 14/08/18

July 24, 2024
blog

Welcome back to CelebMix’s New Music Round Up, where we share with you all of the best releases from the past seven days.

Kicking off this week’s round up is rapper extraordinaire Nicki Minaj, who released her brand new album Queen, which includes the singles ‘Chun-Li’ and ‘Bed’ featuring Ariana Grande.

Summer might be coming to an end but One Bit and Louisa delivered a late entry for anthem of the summer with their collaboration ‘Between You and Me’. Nina Nesbitt also released her new 90’s infused offering,  ‘Loyal To Me’.

Elsewhere, we were treated to new releases from the likes of Troye Sivan, Wesley Stromberg, Sophie Ellis Bextor, With Confidence, Devin Hayes, Young Sires, Sheera, Captain Cuts & Zookeper, Cassadee Pope, and Makk Mikkael.
